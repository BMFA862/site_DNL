Eco-Friendly Project - New MDL

Description : Provide cold drinks like mojitos with local lemons for hot weather.

Provide fresh, cooling beverages such as lemon-based mojitos and natural fruit juices, especially during hot summer months. These drinks, made from fruits grown in the school’s orchard, offer a healthy and refreshing alternative to sugary sodas, keeping students hydrated and energized.
